FAQ

Common questions.

Do you take emergency or urgent calls?

Yes. Emergency electrical calls and urgent troubleshooting are accepted when schedule and availability allow. Call first to confirm availability.

What should I send before scheduling?

Send your name, phone number, job address, a short description of what is happening, and photos if they help explain the issue.

Do you handle commercial work?

Yes. Commercial work includes troubleshooting, tenant improvements, equipment connections, office support, retail support, property support, and punch-list work.

Do you do industrial work?

Yes. Industrial work includes preventive maintenance programs, troubleshooting, electrical panels, repairs, installations, machine installation, pneumatics, and hydraulics.

Can you help with upgrades?

Yes. Upgrade work includes panels, sub panels, dedicated circuits, remodel electrical, EV prep, ADU electrical, garages, and shops.
