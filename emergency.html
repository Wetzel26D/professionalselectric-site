Safety first

If it smells hot, sparks, or trips repeatedly, do not keep testing it.

Call first and describe what happened. If there is active fire, smoke, or immediate danger, contact emergency services before scheduling electrical work.

Urgent FAQ

Before you keep using it.

Should I keep resetting the breaker?

No. If a breaker trips repeatedly, stop resetting it and call to review the symptom.

What should I do if I smell burning?

Stop using the affected circuit or device. If there is smoke, fire, or immediate danger, contact emergency services first.

Can urgent work be scheduled same day?

It depends on availability and the type of issue. Call first so Our team can confirm the best next step.
