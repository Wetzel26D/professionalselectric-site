Privacy Policy

Last updated July 15, 2026.

Information we collect

When you contact Professionals Electric, we may receive information you provide such as your name, phone number, email address, job address, service needs, preferred contact time, and project details.

How information is used

We use this information to review requests, respond to questions, schedule or coordinate electrical work, and communicate about requested services. We do not sell personal information.

Forms and outside services

The website form uses FormSubmit to deliver requests by email. Embedded Google Maps may receive standard technical information from your browser under Google’s own privacy practices. Avoid sending sensitive personal or financial information through the form.

On-device interaction counts

The site may count phone, text, and estimate-button clicks in your browser local storage to help verify that important actions work. These counts remain on your device, do not include form contents or personal information, and are not transmitted by this website.

Retention and security

Service communications may be retained as reasonably needed for scheduling, customer service, project records, and legal obligations. No online transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask about, correct, or request deletion of personal information you previously submitted, subject to legitimate recordkeeping needs.
