Yes. Photos of the panel, outlet, room, or issue can help confirm the best next step.

Do you handle remodel wiring?

Yes. Remodels, additions, garages, shops, and ADU electrical work can be reviewed and scheduled.

What if something feels unsafe?

Call first if there is sparking, burning smell, heat, repeated breaker trips, or partial power loss.
