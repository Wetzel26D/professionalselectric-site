EV FAQ

Quick answers.

Can you wire a Level 2 charger?

Yes. Level 2 charger circuits and EV prep can be reviewed based on the panel, route, charger location, and load.

Do I need the charger already?

No. It helps if you know the model, but Shawn can still review the panel and location first.

What photos help?

Send the panel, the garage or exterior wall route, parking location, and any charger specs you have.
